_faq

Do you build custom music tech solutions or work on existing platforms?
+

Both. We partner with companies to engineer new platforms from scratch or enhance and scale existing systems—from catalog management tools to royalty engines, fan engagement platforms, and more.

Can you help integrate with DSPs, CMOs, and other third-party systems?
+

Absolutely. We specialize in integrating music platforms with Digital Service Providers (DSPs) like Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon, as well as rights organizations, distributors, and reporting systems. Our goal is seamless data flow across your ecosystem.

Do you offer AI/ML services for music platforms?
+

Yes. We’ve worked on everything from AI-driven personalization and recommendations to audio analysis, mood detection, and content moderation. Our AI services brings deep expertise in building production-ready models tailored for music use cases.

Can you help with artist store infrastructure or e-commerce?
+

Yes! We offer services to help manage and scale thousands of artist stores with centralized control over inventory, branding, and storefronts—perfect for label groups, merch companies, and fan platform operators.

Can you help us with rights and royalty management systems?
+

Yes. We’ve worked on systems that calculate royalties from streaming and sales, manage splits across collaborators, and generate reports for labels, publishers, and rights societies. Our solutions ensure transparency, traceability, and compliance.
